The Gap No One Is Solving
Traditional IAM visibility does not extend across AI agent delegation chains.
When an AI agent calls another agent, which calls a third-party API, which touches your customer database — you have no visibility. Most organizations pick one of two broken strategies: lock everything down and kill AI value, or hope nothing breaks. The third option is identity continuity.
The Framework
